# Keel — User Guide

> **Keel** is your all-in-one business finance platform for UK freelancers and small companies.
> It replaces your accountant spreadsheet, your invoicing tool, your expense folder, and your business bank account — in one place.

---

## What problem does Keel solve?

Running a small business or freelancing in the UK involves juggling a lot:

- Chasing invoices and working out who still owes you money
- Keeping receipts and figuring out which expenses are tax deductible
- Estimating your Corporation Tax bill before January
- Calculating the most tax-efficient salary/dividends split
- Making sure your VAT return matches your bank account

Most people use a mix of a personal spreadsheet, a free invoicing website, a shoebox of receipts, and an accountant they see once a year. That costs time and money, and mistakes are common.

**Keel brings everything into one dashboard**, updated in real time, so you always know exactly where your business stands financially.

---

## Who is Keel for?

Keel is built for:

- **Freelancers and contractors** — designers, developers, consultants, writers
- **Limited company directors** — sole-director Ltd companies
- **Small business owners** — 1–10 person teams with a UK company

If you invoice clients, pay expenses, or need to file a UK tax return, Keel is for you.

## Getting started

### 1. Create your account

Visit **keelapp.co.uk** and sign up with your work email address. You will receive a verification email.

### 2. Set up your company

After signing in for the first time you will be asked for:

- **Your company name** — this appears on invoices you send
- **Your company number** (optional) — your Companies House registration number
- **Your VAT number** (optional) — if you are VAT registered

This takes about two minutes. You can update everything later in **Settings**.

### 3. Explore the dashboard

The dashboard gives you an instant snapshot:

| Card | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| Account balance | Your current business account balance |
| Outstanding invoices | Total amount clients owe you right now |
| Overdue invoices | Invoices past their due date |
| Paid this month | Revenue received in the current calendar month |
| Tax estimate | Running Corporation Tax estimate for the year |

## Features

### Banking

Keel gives you a simulated UK business current account with a sort code and account number. You can see every transaction, categorise expenses, and freeze or unfreeze your virtual card.

**How to use it:**

1. Go to **Transactions** in the left sidebar
2. Your account summary shows your balance, plus 30-day income and spend
3. Scroll through your transactions — click any row to see details
4. To change a category, click the transaction and select from the category list
5. To freeze your virtual card, click the **Card** tab and press **Freeze card**

**Transaction categories** match HMRC's allowable expense headings (e.g. Office costs, Travel, Advertising, Professional fees). Correctly categorising your transactions makes your tax return much easier.

---

### Invoicing

Create professional invoices in under a minute, send them by email, and track when clients open them.

**How to create an invoice:**

1. Click **Invoices** in the sidebar, then **New invoice**
2. Enter the client's name, email, and address
3. Add one or more line items — description, quantity, unit price, and VAT rate
4. The subtotal, VAT, and total calculate automatically
5. Set the issue date and due date
6. Click **Create invoice** — it is saved as a **Draft**

**Invoice statuses:**

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Draft | Created but not sent yet — you can still edit it |
| Sent | Emailed to the client |
| Viewed | Client has opened the invoice email |
| Paid | You have marked it as paid |
| Overdue | Past due date and not yet paid |
| Cancelled | Voided — a credit note may be required |

**Sending an invoice:**

- Open the invoice and click **Send invoice**
- Keel emails a PDF to the client automatically
- You can download the PDF yourself from the invoice detail page

**Marking an invoice as paid:**

- Open the invoice and click **Mark as paid**
- Keel records the payment and adds it to your account balance automatically

---

### Receipts & Documents

Keep all your business receipts in one place. Upload a photo or PDF, fill in the details, and link it to the matching transaction.

**How to upload a receipt:**

1. Go to **Documents** in the sidebar
2. Drag and drop a receipt image or PDF onto the upload area, or click to browse
3. Keel stores it securely and opens the receipt detail screen
4. Fill in: **Vendor**, **Amount**, **VAT amount**, **Date**, and **Category**
5. Click **Save receipt details**

**Linking a receipt to a transaction:**

Once you have saved the receipt details:

1. Click **Match to transaction** on the receipt detail page
2. Search for the matching transaction by description, merchant, or amount
3. Click the transaction — the receipt is now linked

Linked receipts are stored against your transactions as evidence for HMRC. HMRC requires you to keep receipts for **6 years** (Corporation Tax) or **4 years** (VAT).

**Supported file types:** JPEG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC (iPhone), PDF — up to 20 MB each.

> **Coming soon:** The **✦ Extract with AI** button will automatically read your receipt and fill in all the fields for you. This feature is in development.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. All data is stored in the UK, encrypted at rest and in transit. Keel is built to GDPR requirements.

**Is the banking account real?**
In the current version, Keel uses a simulated account seeded with demo transactions so you can explore all the features. A real account backed by a payment provider (open banking integration) will be added in a future release.

**Can I use Keel on my phone?**
A mobile app for iOS and Android is in development. The web app at keelapp.co.uk works well on mobile browsers in the meantime.

**Do I still need an accountant?**
Keel does not provide regulated financial advice. It gives you accurate, real-time data so that if you do use an accountant, your meetings are quicker and cheaper. Many freelancers find that Keel gives them everything they need to file their own returns via HMRC's online portal.

**What VAT rates are supported?**
Standard rate (20%), reduced rate (5%), and zero rate (0%).

**Can I send invoices in currencies other than GBP?**
Not yet — GBP only in the current version.
